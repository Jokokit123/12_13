Synopsis

The story follows two siblings, Jad and Cally, as they traverse a universe filled with wonder and destruction.
Jad, the younger brother, is brimming with emotion and curiosity about the worlds they explore.
His older sister, Cally, plays the role of the "sweet" and "caring" sibling, but her warmth is a facade.
In truth, she lacks emotions and mimics them only to comfort Jad.


Together, they journey across worlds, staying long enough for Jad to admire their
beauty — only for Cally to destroy them afterward, simply for "fun".
Both siblings are beings of corruption, wielding the forbidden elemental branch of the Tree of Elements,
a mystical structure encompassing nature, light, darkness, and a force so taboo it is never named.


The game is set in Abstracia, a fantastical world of elements and the equivalent of Earth.
Jad becomes enamored with its beauty and, on his supposed birthday, begs Cally not to destroy it.
She agrees, but under one condition: Jad must prove himself worthy of protecting the world.
He is given one year to train, face her, and demonstrate his strength and resolve,
as he meets powerful companions and friends, preparing for the challenges that lie ahead.


Structure

The story unfolds over 12 chapters, corresponding to each month of the year.
These chapters include essential story quests that prepare you for the final battle.
By deepening your friendships with your companions, you unlock powerful tag-team attacks!
Completing their respective story quests also rewards you with special equipment
that enhances their mechanics and mitigate their weaknesses.

Character Info

The game features seven playable characters: Jad and six others he meets along his journey.
Each character comes with unique mechanics for casting skills and synergizing with the party,
abilities you’ll need to master to reach the end. Beyond combat, every character also has overworld
mechanics to assist with exploration, puzzle-solving, and traversal.
